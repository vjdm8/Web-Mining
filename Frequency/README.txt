counter.py requires the installation of the
folllowing libraries:

re
time
nltk with stopword corpus
urllib2
operator

It also requires the attached 
negative-words and positive-words txt files.

Finally it will read the text within the textfile.txt file
and return the words that are present in 4 or more senti-sentences.
(senti-sentences are those sentences that have a positive or negative
sentiment)
